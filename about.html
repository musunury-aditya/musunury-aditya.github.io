About Aditya Musunury

Aditya Musunury is a Registered Physiotherapy Resident (Provisional Practice) with the College of Physiotherapists of Ontario. He has completed the PCE Written Component and is scheduled to take the Ontario Clinical Exam (OCE) in June 2026 for full independent practice.

He brings over 8 years of clinical experience across musculoskeletal, orthopedic, neurological, and cardiopulmonary rehabilitation. His background combines hands-on patient care, exercise prescription, manual therapy, and patient education.

Before moving into the Ontario practice pathway, he owned and managed three physiotherapy clinics in India, where he led teams, oversaw operations, and delivered evidence-based physiotherapy services to patients across multiple specialties.

His current goal is to contribute as a part-time supervised physiotherapist within a multidisciplinary clinic setting in Ontario, while continuing to build on a strong foundation of patient-centered, outcome-focused care.
